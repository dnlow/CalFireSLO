Station 35, Las Tablas Fire Station

275 Cypress Mountain Drive
Paso Robles, CA 93446
(805) 238-4592

Station 35 is located on the southern edge of Lake Nacimiento, Las Tablas Station 35 is in operation each summer during declared fire season. The Las Tablas response area is considered “high risk” with the combination of thick brush, steep mountains and little fire history.

Station 35 staffing includes a Fire Captain/Operator and two or three firefighters. Station 35 responds on a CAL FIRE ICS Type-III four wheel drive fire engine.
