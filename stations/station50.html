Station 50, Creston Fire Station

6055 Webster Rd
Creston, CA 93422
(805) 238-4343

Creston Fire Station 50 is located east of the City of Atascadero, in the town of Creston. Station 50 is owned by the County of San Luis Obispo, and operates an ICS Type-I engine with a two Fire Captain and two Fire Apparatus Engineer one Firefighter II. Station 50 operates a 10 member Paid-Call Firefighter program. Station 50 is home to Water Tender 50, a ICS Type I Water tender.
