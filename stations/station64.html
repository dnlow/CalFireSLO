Station 64, Pismo Beach Fire Station

990 Bello St.

805-773-7031

Pismo Beach is a full-service, oceanfront city with a population of about 8,500, located in San Luis Obispo County, midway between Los Angeles and San Francisco. The City's commercial base is primarily tourism-oriented, with a significant number of hotels, motels and restaurants. The climate is mild year-round with many days of sunshine and warm beach weather. There is no smog, which makes the area healthy for outdoor activities.

The City of Pismo Beach Fire Department in a cooperative fire protection agreement with Cal Fire to employs a full-time staff including a Battalion Chief, three Fire Captains, three Fire Apparatus Engineers and an Administrative Assistant. Additionally, the department employs 20 reserve/paid call firefighters and 18 seasonal lifeguards and junior lifeguard program instructors.

For additional information regarding the fire department or possible employment opportunities please email fire@pismobeach.org or visit the Pismo Beach Fire page on the City of Pismo Beach website.
