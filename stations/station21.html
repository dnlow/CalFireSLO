Station 21, San Luis Obispo Airport

4671 Broad Street
San Luis Obispo, CA 93401
(805) 544-5105

One of the newest CAL FIRE/San Luis Obispo County Fire Department facilities is located at San Luis Obispo Regional Airport. Station 21 professional service includes fire protection for the Edna Valley, rural areas surrounding the City of San Luis Obispo as well as automatic aid with the City of San Luis Obispo. Station 21 houses many specialize pieces of equipment including an Engine, Rescue, Crash Truck, Water Tender and MCI vehicle are staffed by permanent personnel with the rescue and water tender served by a very active and dedicated 25 person Paid Call Fire Company.
